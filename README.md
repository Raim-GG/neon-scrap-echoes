# Neon Scrap Echoes

## Game Description
Neon Scrap Echoes is an immersive action-adventure game set in a vibrant neon-infused world. Players take on the role of a skilled scavenger navigating through the remnants of a once-thriving civilization, uncovering secrets and fighting against fierce enemies.

## How to Play
1. **Character Movement**: Use the arrow keys or WASD to move your character around the environment.
2. **Interactions**: Press the 'E' key to interact with objects and NPCs.
3. **Combat**: Engage in combat with enemies using a range of weapons and special abilities.
4. **Quests**: Complete various quests to uncover the story and improve your character's skills.

## Features
- **Stunning Visuals**: Experience a unique art style with neon graphics and dynamic lighting.
- **Engaging Combat System**: Utilize a combination of weapons and abilities for thrilling battles.
- **Exploration**: Discover a vast open world filled with hidden treasures and secrets.
- **Character Customization**: Customize your character's appearance and abilities as you progress through the game.
- **Dynamic Storytelling**: Make choices that affect the outcome of the story and how NPCs react to you.
